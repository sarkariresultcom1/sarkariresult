Result FAQs
When will UPSSSC release the PET Result 2025?
The result has been declared on 05 December 2025. It is now available online.
Will UPSSSC send the result by SMS or email?
No, candidates must check the result online on the official website.
What if I forget my registration number?
You can retrieve it using the "Forgot Registration" option on the website by entering your name and father's name or mobile number.
Can I download the PET scorecard later?
Yes, the scorecard remains available for a year (usually until the next PET), but it is highly recommended to download and save a copy immediately.
