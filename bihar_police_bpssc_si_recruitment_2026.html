How to Apply Online for Bihar Police BPSSC SI 2026?

1. Visit the official BPSSC recruitment portal directly or via the Important Links section below.

2. Complete the initial registration process using your basic details (Mobile No, Email ID).

3. Login using your generated credentials.

4. Fill the online application form carefully. Make sure your name and DOB match your 10th certificate.

5. Upload scanned copies of required documents: Graduation Certificate, Aadhaar Card, Passport Size Photograph, Signature, Caste/Domicile Certificate, and Thumb Impression.

6. Pay the required application fee (₹100) securely through the online payment gateway.

7. Submit the application form and download the final confirmation page for future reference.
