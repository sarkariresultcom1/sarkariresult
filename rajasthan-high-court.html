RHC Stenographer Recruitment FAQs

Q1. How many vacancies are available in Rajasthan High Court Stenographer Recruitment 2026?

There are 163 vacancies for Stenographer Grade III (Hindi & English) for both TSP and Non-TSP areas.

Q2. When will the online application start?

The online application process starts on 22 July 2026 and the last date to apply is 10 August 2026.

Q3. What is the educational qualification?

Candidates must have passed 12th (Intermediate) from a recognized board along with O Level, COPA, Diploma in Computer Application, or RSCIT certificate.

Q4. What is the age limit for the post?

Candidates should be between 18 to 40 years as on 01 January 2027. Reserved category candidates will get age relaxation as per government rules.

Q5. What is the application fee for General category?

The application fee for General, OBC, and Other State candidates is ₹750. For OBC (NCL)/EWS it is ₹600, and for SC/ST it is ₹450.
