How to Download Bihar BPSC 72nd Pre Admit Card 2026?

1. Visit the official BPSC website directly or use the Important Link given below.

2. Go to the "Admit Card" or "Notice Board" section on the homepage.

3. Click on the link designated for BPSC Integrated 72nd CCE Pre Admit Card 2026.

4. A login page will appear. Enter your Registration Number and Password (or Date of Birth).

5. Enter the captcha code and click on submit.

6. Your BPSC 72nd Hall Ticket will be displayed on the screen.

7. Download the admit card and print multiple copies for future use and to carry to the examination center.
